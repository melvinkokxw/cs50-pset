Chilli Oil

Sichuan style chilli oil

Ingredients

  • Chilli Flakes
  • Oil
  • Star Anise
  • Garlic
  • Coriander Seeds
  • Cinnamon
  • Sichuan Peppercorns
  • Black Cardomom
  • Salt
  • Black Vinegar

Steps

  1. Mix all ths spices
  2. Add oil to spices and simmer for 2 hours
  3. Mix chilli flakes, salt and black vinegar
  4. Add part of oil into chilli flakes mixture and stir well
  5. Add remaining oil into chilli flakes mixture
  6. Bottle in glass jar
