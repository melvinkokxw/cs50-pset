Kimi no Shoyuzuke

Egg yolks marinated in soy sauce

Ingredients

  • Egg Yolks
  • Soy Sauce
  • Mirin

Steps

  1. Add egg yolks to bowl/container
  2. Add soy sauce and mirin in 2:1 ratio to egg yolks, at least enough to cover 2/3 of the yolks
  3. Cover bowl/container with plastic wrap
  4. Keep in fridge for a few days
  5. Retrieve egg yolk and serve over rice
